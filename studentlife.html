Student Life

Location and Area


The Murray Student Center is the heart of student life at Marist. Recognized as one of the 50 Best College Student Centers, it brings together key resources, services, and spaces that support and engage the campus community. Inside, you’ll find the main dining hall, wellness center, Student Government Association offices, recital hall and theatre, student lounges, and versatile meeting rooms. It’s a central spot where students can relax, connect, and take part in a wide range of events and activities throughout the year.

The Hudson River Valley is a region full of natural beauty, cultural diversity, and historic charm. With everything from scenic landscapes to vibrant towns and locally sourced food, it’s an exciting place to explore and experience. Marist College is right at the center of it all, offering students the chance to be part of a proud and historic community. Marist has built strong partnerships with major institutions like IBM, the Franklin D. Roosevelt Presidential Library, and the Institute of Ecosystem Studies. These collaborations give students valuable opportunities to engage with leaders in technology, history, and environmental science. The college also offers unique academic experiences through innovative programs such as the Marist Institute for Public Opinion and the Marist Bureau of Economic Research.

Social Life and Accommodations

With over 100 clubs and organizations, both on and off campus, and an extremely present student body, on campus life is incredible. Located in the beautiful Hudson River Valley, which offers an outstanding environment for living, learning, and growing. The campus community is vibrant and welcoming, with a wide range of resources designed to support students academically, socially, and personally. From modern residence halls and diverse dining options to recreation facilities and comfortable spaces to connect with others, Marist makes it easy to get involved and feel at home. The college also provides strong support for students with different learning needs or physical challenges. Above all, Marist prioritizes student safety and wellbeing, with dedicated staff working to maintain a secure and supportive atmosphere for all.
