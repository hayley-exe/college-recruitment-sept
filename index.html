Marist University, located in Poughkeepsie, New York, is a private institution that blends liberal arts education with strong professional programs, especially in the arts, communication, fashion, and digital media. Known for its scenic Hudson River campus and a branch in Florence, Italy, Marist offers students hands-on learning in fields like studio art, graphic design, media studies, and fashion merchandising. The university emphasizes creativity and innovation, with cutting-edge facilities such as the Steel Plant Studios and the Media Center supporting student work. In 2025, it transitioned from Marist College to Marist University, reflecting its growing academic and global reach.
